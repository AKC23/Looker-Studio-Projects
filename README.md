# Looker Studio Projects

## Project 1: Ecommerce Data Analysis: Retention, Revenue & Performance Insights 

**Description:**
The project leverages Looker Studio to analyze eCommerce data, focusing on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as customer retention, revenue trends, and overall business performance. The dashboard integrates various CSV datasets to provide actionable insights for improving customer engagement, retention strategies, and boosting revenue growth. It presents visualizations like cohort analysis, AOV vs. revenue per segment, and churn rate to guide data-driven decision-making.

### Goals: 
- Develop a comprehensive and automated dashboard using Looker Studio to track eCommerce KPIs.
- Analyze customer retention, repeat purchase rate, churn rate, and AOV trends.
- Perform cohort analysis to understand customer acquisition and retention patterns.
- Integrate Google Analytics 4 data to enhance business performance analysis.
- Provide actionable insights to drive strategic decision-making in customer retention and revenue generation.

### Project Insights:

1. **Customer Segmentation:** The analysis highlights the significance of VIP and High CLV segments, emphasizing their role in driving a significant portion of the revenue.
2. **Engagement Gaps:** Despite a large subscriber base, conversion efficiency remains low, indicating the need for improved segmentation and email marketing strategies.
3. **Retention Focus:** The project identifies retention as a key driver of revenue, noting a decline in retention rates in 2025, which demands focused churn mitigation efforts.
4. **Discount Strategy:** It provides insights into the negative correlation between discounts and AOV, suggesting the need for more targeted discounting strategies.
5. **Seasonal Trends:** The analysis reveals strong seasonal performance, particularly in Q4, but highlights a sharp decline in 2025, raising concerns about customer attrition or external macroeconomic factors.
